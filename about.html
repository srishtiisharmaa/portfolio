Introduction

I am Srishti Sharma, a versatile graphic designer with a passion for crafting visually compelling digital experiences. With a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts from George Brown College and a postgraduate certification in Web Development, I have honed my skills in graphic design, UI/UX design, and web development. Over the years, I have collaborated with leading digital marketing firms and advertising agencies, where I played key roles in creating engaging social media content, impactful advertisements, and dynamic webpages for international clients. My proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, coupled with my dedication to meeting project deadlines and exceeding client expectations, has enabled me to deliver high-quality design solutions consistently. Fluent in English, Hindi, and proficient in German, I bring a multicultural perspective to my work, ensuring designs resonate with diverse audiences. I am always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies and design trends, striving to push the boundaries of creativity in every project I undertake.
